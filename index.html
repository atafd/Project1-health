Take Control: 5 Powerful Prevention Strategies

Practical Steps for Blood Pressure Management
DASH Diet Eating Plan - NHLBI Recommended Proportions
🥗
DASH Diet Revolution
Follow the Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension - rich in fruits, vegetables, and low-fat dairy while limiting sodium to 2,300mg daily.
Pro Tip: Use herbs and spices instead of salt. One teaspoon of salt = 2,300mg sodium!
🏃‍♂️
Active Lifestyle
150 minutes of moderate exercise weekly can lower systolic BP by 5-8 mmHg. Think brisk walking, cycling, or swimming.
Pro Tip: Break it down: 30 minutes, 5 days a week. Even 10-minute sessions count!
⚖️
Weight Management
Losing just 5-10% of body weight can significantly reduce blood pressure. Target BMI under 25.
Pro Tip: Measure waist circumference: Men < 40 inches, Women < 35 inches.
🚫
Sodium Intelligence
Read labels! 75% of sodium comes from processed foods. Cook fresh and choose low-sodium options.
Pro Tip: Watch for "hidden" sodium in bread, cheese, and canned soups.
🧘‍♀️
Stress Mastery
Chronic stress raises blood pressure. Practice mindfulness, deep breathing, or meditation for 10 minutes daily.
Pro Tip: Try the 4-7-8 breathing technique: Inhale 4s, hold 7s, exhale 8s.
